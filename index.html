THE BEAGLE BLOG

 The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound,similar in appearance to the much
larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting here,known as
beagling.possessing a great sense aof smell and superior tracking instincts,the
beagle is the primary breed used as detection dog for prohibited agricultural
imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world.The beagle is intelligent
and is a popular pet due to its size,good temper,and a lack of inherited health
problems.
